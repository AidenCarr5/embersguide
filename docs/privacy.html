Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Embers Tracker is a local desktop application for Girl Guides and Embers leaders. The app helps leaders track unit roster information, attendance, badge progress, planning, patrol points, and cookie tracking information.

Information Stored

The app stores tracker information entered by the user, such as Ember names, patrol/year details, attendance, badge progress, meeting plans, patrol points, and cookie tracker entries.

Local Storage

Tracker data is stored locally on the user's device unless the user chooses to sync a tracker file with Google Drive.

Google Drive Sync

If Google Drive sync is used, the app creates, opens, and updates a selected tracker JSON file in the user's Google Drive. The app uses the Google Drive drive.file permission so it only accesses files created by the app or files the user explicitly selects through Google Picker.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell user data. Tracker files are shared only when a user chooses to share the selected Google Drive file with another Google account.

Third-Party Services

Google sign-in and Google Drive are used for optional file sync. Google processes authentication and Drive access according to Google's own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner at the support email configured on the Google OAuth consent screen.
